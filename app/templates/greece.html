Greece

I went to Greece while I was studying abroad in Italy. I went on my own to the island of Rhodes. I have to be honest - I did it wrong. I went by myself to a little island with not enough books on me and booked myself in a room with a broken TV. So, after I had seen everything there was to see (in about 2 days), I spent the next 3 bored out of my mind. Still, I'm glad I went and had the experience. After all, I learned how to not travel!

I visited the Old Town neighborhood, which had the really cool Palace of the Grand Master of the Knights of Rhodes, which was surrounded by people and clearly very popular.

I also saw the really old Turkish mosque known as the Suleymaniye Mosque. I didn't get to go inside, but it was cool to learn about and was very pretty.

My other adventures in Rhodes led me to the Acropolis and to a really small aquarium at the tip of the island. The aquarium was actually pretty cute. It was a pretty little island, but I hope to see more of Greece in the future (gotta go to Athens!).
